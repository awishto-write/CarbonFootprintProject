What is a Carbon Footprint?

  • A carbon footprint is the total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions caused by an individual, event, service, organization, place, or product.
  • It is expressed as a carbon dioxide equivalent (CO2e).
  • Calculate your carbon footprint to determine how much greenhouse gas emission you contribute to, and learn how to reduce it!
